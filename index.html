About Us

Welcome to The Erie Beach Hotel in the heart of Port Dover, Ontario, Canada. We are located just steps from the popular Port Dover beach on the north shore of Lake Erie, and are minutes away from other local attractions that thousands come to enjoy, such as the Lighthouse Festival Theatre, the Port Dover Harbour Museum, a romantic pier, many one-of-a-kind shops and boutiques, and the ever-growing Friday the 13th rallies.

The Restaurant

We have an option for everyone, whether you’re looking for a full service dining experience, or a great place to simply grab a drink. We offer two separate dining areas, The Cove Room and The Terrace Room, (which features our newly completed Perch Patio), as well as private meeting/dining rooms and 18 quaint and newly renovated motel-style rooms..

Us

Our family-run business has been famously serving Lake Erie Perch and Pickerel for over 70 years. Treat yourself to one of our amazing restaurants or to an overnight stay in our appealing and comfortable rooms; you deserve it! Our family feels it’s important to support our community and are proud to serve local products in both of our dining areas, from local wine & beer to fish, meat & produce.

Hotel

Located one block from the beach and harbour-front, our hotel is the perfect location for your getaway! Relax, rejuvenate, and recharge with us; you deserve it! We have 18 comfortable motel-style. All rooms are non-smoking and pet-free. We take great pride in the upkeep of our building, and our rooms receive small updates each year.

The Restaurant

Allow us to take you on a culinary journey in one of our restaurants. We have something for everyone! We offer full service dining in the Cove Room, or casual dining in the Terrace Room (Upstairs.) We encourage you to try our famous Erie Beach perch & celery bread, but you can also enjoy a quick drink and appetizer or go for the full dining experience.
